Welcome to SplashWorks!

We at SplashWorks have just released our new game award-winnning Grape Juice City.

Choose your juicer, paint the town with fruit, and win the hearts of Grape Juice City on this hot summer day!

Grape Juice City is a math-based party shooter, where you play as tropical birds that splash pedestrians with drinks from your juice stand to cool them off! Bounce around a tropical metropolis while you gather fruit and recyclables, craft them into juicy drinks, and toss them to the sun-loving people of Grape Juice City in this friendly but competitive party game for all ages!

Now splash on over to Steam to play Grape Juice City for FREE!

Award-Winning Game

Grape Juice City was a three time award winning game at SGX in 2022 and 2023!
